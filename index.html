WKC Graduation 2020 Formal Event

This event is FREE to all WKC 2020 Grade 12 Graduates! It is a chance for graduates to get together with all their classmates one last time. There will be a group photo on the front steps, a Grand March across the stage, speeches and areas set up to take photos with your friends to celebrate the culmination of all your hard work over the past 12 years.

Details will follow but we anticipate the event to start between 6:00pm and 7:00pm and then end around 10:30pm. We wanted to get the word out so that everyone can start planning for the event.

Due to Sask Health Authority restrictions and the number of people allowed to be together inside a building, this event is ONLY for the graduates; no parents, no guests, no escorts. We are working with the Conexus Arts Center to set up live streaming of the event. We also have photographers attending to take photos of everyone as they cross the stage which will then be made available to download from the website.

Grad Committee

Welcome to the WKC 2020 Graduation Banquet website! This event is organized and run by parent volunteers who want all grade 12 students to have the opportunity to attend a celebration culminating their time from pre-school to grade 12. We want these students to celebrate their success with family and friends.

The parent volunteers will work hard this next year to put on a memorable evening for students and families. Please help us spread the word to other WKC grade 12 families about this website as it will be a great place to find information about the event, fundraising, tickets and pictures from the banquet.
