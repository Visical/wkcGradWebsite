There were so many photos taken that we have to post them in two different groupings.

The first group of photos are currently uploaded and will remain on the website for you to download until Thursday, September 10, 2020. We will then remove that group of photos.

The second group of photos will be uploaded on Friday, September 11, 2020 and will remain on the website for you to download until Monday, September 21, 2020.

To download photos, click on 'Photos' on the left side of the screen. Enter the password. You will then be able to click on each of the links to download the pictures (or just the ones you want). This will download a zip file of all the pictures in that link to your computer. You can then delete the one's you do not want.

Grad Committee

Welcome to the WKC 2020 Graduation Banquet website! This event is organized and run by parent volunteers who want all grade 12 students to have the opportunity to attend a celebration culminating their time from pre-school to grade 12. We want these students to celebrate their success with family and friends.

The parent volunteers will work hard this next year to put on a memorable evening for students and families. Please help us spread the word to other WKC grade 12 families about this website as it will be a great place to find information about the event, fundraising, tickets and pictures from the banquet.
