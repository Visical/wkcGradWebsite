2020 WKC Graduate Event
Friday, August 21, 2020 at 6:00 pm
Conexus Arts Center

Click here to watch the live stream event.

Due to COVID restrictions this is a STUDENT ONLY event. Masks will be provided and we encourage all Graduates to wear masks. No in/out privileges – if you leave the venue you will not be allowed back in. Anyone causing problems will be asked to leave.

Itinerary of Event

6:00 pm – Doors open at the Conexus Arts Center and sign in begins
6:00 pm – Photo opportunities inside (bring your phones/camera)
7:15 pm – Grand March - live streaming will begin. Go to the website and click on the link to watch.
8:30 pm – Speeches will follow the Grand March
9:00 pm – Group photo

Details

The venue doors open at 6:00 pm and sign in will begin. Your name must be on the student list to enter. Your forehead temperature will be taken using a no-touch thermometer and admittance is not allowed if your temperature is over 100.6°F (38°C). Each student will receive a bag that includes a mask and small bottle of hand sanitizer (the venue has sanitizer on site as well).

Graduates will have 45 minutes to move around and take photos at the 3 areas set up inside for photo opportunities. Each area will have a Committee Member present to assist with taking photos as these photos will be taken using your own camera. We ask that kids be respectful of others and move on to the different photo areas in a timely manner to ensure all students who want to have their pictures taken are able to.

At 6:45 pm Graduates will be asked to pair up with someone they are comfortable sitting next to and then take a seat in preparation for the Grand March. Charis are set up in pairs with the required social distance between each pair of chairs.

The Grand March will start at 7:15 pm and a short program will follow.

Following the Grand March and program there will be a few group photos.

Important Information

The Grand March and program will be live streamed and can be watched by coming to the website and clicking on the link that will be posted on the home screen the day of the event. We will try to start it as close to 7:15 pm as we can (it will not start before 7:15 pm but it might be a bit after).

There will be photographers on site taking pictures of the Graduates during the Grand March and also for the group photos at the end. These photos will be uploaded to the website and password protected so only people with the password will be able to view, download and then print off the size and quantity they want. After a short period of time all the photos will be removed from the website. The password will be in the bag given to each Graduate in attendance.

If you do not want your child's photos uploaded on the website please notify us IMMIDIATELY via email at WKCGradEvent@outlook.com and also advise your child to NOT stand with all the other Graduates for the group photos.


We anticipate the event being concluded around 9:00 - 9:30pm.

Grad Committee

Welcome to the WKC 2020 Graduation Banquet website! This event is organized and run by parent volunteers who want all grade 12 students to have the opportunity to attend a celebration culminating their time from pre-school to grade 12. We want these students to celebrate their success with family and friends.

The parent volunteers will work hard this next year to put on a memorable evening for students and families. Please help us spread the word to other WKC grade 12 families about this website as it will be a great place to find information about the event, fundraising, tickets and pictures from the banquet.
