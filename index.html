It is with a heavy heart that we announce the 2020 WKC Graduation Banquet and Event has officially been cancelled.
We have received confirmation from Evraz that they are unable to allow the event to happen due to the health restrictions still in place.
Since we can no longer host the event all tickets will be fully refunded.
If you paid for your tickets via an e-transfer, you will receive your refund via e-transfer. If a security question is required for us to do the e-transfer, we will set it so the password is wkcgrad.
If you paid cash for your tickets, we will be reaching out to you via the email and/or phone number provided on the Ticket Purchase Form to set up the refund.
Note – if you purchased additional tickets once we opened up the number of tickets allowed (so made two separate payments) we will be refunding the whole amount in one transaction.
This is going to take us a bit of time to get through everyone so please be patient. If you have not heard from us by the end of June, then please reach out to us via email at WKCGradEvent@outlook.com.


So now on a positive note!

With the fundraising that was done we are we do have some money left over and we are planning to put that money back towards something for the graduates. We do not have any details at this time and are still trying to figure out what can be done while staying within the Provincial Health restrictions for group gatherings but we are working on a few ideas and possibilities.
Keep watching our website, Facebook page and Instagram for updates. Hopefully we can pull something informal and fun together for anyone who is interested and able to attend.

Grad Committee

Welcome to the WKC 2020 Graduation Banquet website! This event is organized and run by parent volunteers who want all grade 12 students to have the opportunity to attend a celebration culminating their time from pre-school to grade 12. We want these students to celebrate their success with family and friends.

The parent volunteers will work hard this next year to put on a memorable evening for students and families. Please help us spread the word to other WKC grade 12 families about this website as it will be a great place to find information about the event, fundraising, tickets and pictures from the banquet.

With respect to COVID-19, we want to let you know that we have been in contact with Evraz Place and will continue to monitor the situation as it unfolds.
